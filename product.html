NBA 2K22

When a basketball player gets into “the zone,” They can see things before they happen, every little detail about their opponent stands out, and the rim seems to get bigger and more inviting. NBA 2K22 never quite managed to get me all the way into the zone, but the improved gameplay and breadth of content of this iteration get it closer than it’s been for the past few years. 2K22 feels like a breath of fresh air compared to 2K21.
